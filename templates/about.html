The Bible Study Blog

This blog was created to support and encourage those who desire to grow in faith by the study of the Holy Scriptures. Far to many believers who have routinely attended churches, have been left destitute of knowledge concerning how to live a holy lifestyle. This is largely due to the fact that churches have become places where entertainment rules the order of the day, instead of study of the Holy scriptures and TRUE WORSHIP! This blog is to let those who are sincere worshippers know that you are not alone in your journey to know The Most High in Spirit and Truth!
